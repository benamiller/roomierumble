Tournament Format


League of Legends

Each match will be a Best of 1. Order to be determined during the tournament.

    Group Stage

    • Game 1: Team A vs Team B, Team C vs Team D
    • Game 2: Team A vs Team C, Team B vs Team D
    • Game 3: Team A vs Team D, Team B vs Team C

    Deciders

    • Game 4: Bottom two teams play for third place
    • Game 5: Top two teams play for first place

NBA 2K

NBA 2K will be played in a round-robin format. Each team plays each other once. Standard game settings, 5 minute quarters.

  • Game Mode: MyTEAM Friendly Match
  • Points: 2 for win, 0 for loss
  • Tie Breakers: Head-to-head, then point differential

CS2

CS2 matches are Best of 1 on a pre-determined map pool. MR12 format. One map ban per team. Teams will play in a group stage followed by playoffs.

  • Game Mode: Competitive 5v5, MR12
  • Map Pool: Dust II, Mirage, Inferno
  • Points: Based on group stage ranking

Trackmania

Trackmania will feature a series of cup mode races on custom maps. Points awarded based on final ranking in each cup.

  • Game Mode: Cup Mode (Points Limit)
  • Tracks: 5 custom tracks
  • Points: Top 8 players per race score points (10, 8, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1)
